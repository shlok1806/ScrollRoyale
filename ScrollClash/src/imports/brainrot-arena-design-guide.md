🧠 CHARACTER DESIGN – THE BRAIN MASCOT

This is very important.

The brain should NOT be a static realistic brain.

It must be:

Slightly cartoonish

Cute but chaotic

Small expressive eyes

Tiny small arms/hands

Floating slightly above a small glowing platform

Idle breathing animation (subtle bounce)

As rot level increases during the day:

Eyes slowly get smaller

Eyelids droop

Color shifts slightly toward purple/pink

Small cracks appear

One fly appears at 50% rot

Two flies orbit at 75% rot

Brain slightly slouches

Hands hang downward when near max rot

At low rot:

Eyes wide open

Slight sparkle

Confident posture

Include animation notes in design:

Idle bounce 2–3px

Flies rotate slowly

Eye blink every few seconds

Progress ring glows stronger at low rot, flickers slightly at high rot

This brain is the personality of the app.
